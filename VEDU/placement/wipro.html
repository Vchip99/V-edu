Wipro

Wipro is a global IT service provider. The company is headquartered in Bangalore, India. The main function of the company is that it works as an IT consulting and outsourcing service company. The company also provides with deep technology expertise and a vertically aligned business model.

CEO: Abidali Neemuchwala

Founder: M.H. Hasham Premji

Founded: 29 December 1945, India

Headquarters: Bengaluru

Revenue: 8.5 billion USD (2017)

official Website : http://www.wipro.com

Selection Process:

The company conducts recruitment process every year to select new candidates. The selection process of the company consists of 3 rounds. These rounds are as follow

  • Written Exam
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

Academic Criteria:

  • Above 50 percent marks in Class X and XII and 60 percent or more in B.Tech.
  • No backlogs are allowed.
  • Gap of upto two years is allowed with valid reasons.

Interview Experience 1

Wipro is one of the top most company in the world. We had a campus drive in our college on 24 & 25th October 2016. At first, there was a PPT presented by one of the Senior HR in our auditorium. AMCAT conducted the written test.

The total students were divided into 2 sections.
1. Toppers and Non- Toppers. I was among the toppers Section.

Written Test:

Toppers Criteria (Career should be > 75%):

1. Technical (12 questions 15 mins)
2. Essay Writing (Only if you qualify in Technical u will be able to attend this). Essay writing is so easy just to check spelling mistakes and typing speed guys please don't copy and paste it from INTERNET. (Strict advice)

Non -Toppers:

1. Aptitude, Logical, English (48 questions I guess).
2. Technical.
3. Essay Writing.

Cut off for toppers 7/12 (Not sure).

If you qualify the written test then you will be called for the Technical Interview if one qualifies this then HR interview. Total out of 260 students in the toppers section 110 qualified and out of 280 in non-toppers section 86 students qualified in the written test.

My interview experience:

Technical Interview:

Wipro's technical interview is not so easy you need to have some preparation if you are a CSE/IT guy and for others some basic programs and knowledge on C& C++.

Hr: Tell me about yourself but with one condition Do not repeat a single word that you have written in your CV.

Me: I said it but I was not satisfied myself. I was bit nervous while answering this but after this, it went finely.

Hr: Why IT?
Me: Explained.

Hr: Told to write a program.
Me: Done & explained.

Hr: Fav. Subject and many technical Questions from my branch subjects (DATA STRUCTURES NETWORKS, OS) not a single question from C & C++.
Me: Except 1 question I have answered all the questions.

There were some HR questions and from your CV, so go through your CV properly.

Hr: Asked any questions for him?
Me: Asked.

Hr: He gave handshake and told nice to meet you.
Me: Thank you, Sir.

My interview went for 40 minutes.

The HR who interviewed me was one of the coolest person I have ever met. I passed the technical interview then within a few minutes I was called for HR round.

HR ROUND:

Basic HR questions about yourself, family, Why Wipro, Relocation, etc...

This is so easy they just check your communication skills and presence of mind, attitude.

A total of 126 students qualified in Technical interview out of 187. Then Finally 114 qualified the HR Round. So Basically HR round is also an elimination round.

My advice: Be confident in yourself and try to attend the interview. Don't bluff, speak the truth and always maintain a smile on your face and keep an eye contact with the HR.

Results came after 1 week my name was there in the list and that was the happiest moment.

ALL THE BEST GUYS.

Interview Experience 2


There will be three rounds in Wipro selection process.

(1) Written test.
(2) Technical round.
(3) HR round.

(1) Written test:

Wipro came to our campus on 12th September. Actually, Wipro selection process is of two types.

1)Topper's criteria.
2)General criteria.

The difference between the two is in topper's criteria in return test they have to take only Programming test and essay writing only whereas remaining has to attempt.

1) Verbal ability.
2) Quantitative ability.
3) Reasoning ability.
4) Programming test.

If you qualified in all above sections then you get essay writing on screen otherwise, you can leave. In that situation when my time is running out I got tense whether I can get an essay or I have to leave. In that situation, I got essay writing on a screen. After writing essay they asked us to wait in auditorium for results. And I was shortlisted for technical interview. They asked us to come at 9:30 the next day.

(2) Technical Round:

This is the major part of the selection, in which our skills are tested. The person who is from non-IT can relax up to some extent. They had to be thorough with basics of C and Data Structures. Students who are from IT domain had to be thorough with C, Data Structures, OOP concepts and your areas of interest such as DBMS and Operating Systems. For me, interviewer asked me some concepts related to C and C++. And then he tested me how I was with DBMS and asked more than 15 questions related to keys, constraints, definitions and more queries. Later he asked some concepts on operating systems while he asking I said him that I was not comforted with OS concepts. After that by asking some questions related to general topics, he congratulated me that I was selected for HR round.

(3) HR round:

It is very simple round that we can succeed it mainly with our smile, here you can face some questions such as,

1) Tell me about yourself?

2) Why should I hire you?

3) Why Wipro?

4) Are you planning for your higher studies?

5) Strengths and weaknesses.

6) Hobbies etc.

While answering such questions you have to be active and spontaneous. Don't eliminate your smile. After answering all questions, she asked me any queries. I asked about company's environment and she explained. Then she asked me to leave.

Finally, results are out after two weeks and I got shortlisted for Wipro which makes me feel happy.
