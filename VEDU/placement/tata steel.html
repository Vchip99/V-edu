Tata Steel

Tata Steel Limited is a part of Tata Group, headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. The company founded country's first industrial city, Jamshedpur, where Asia's first integrated steel plant has been established. Founded in 1907, Tata steel is one of the top ten global steel industries which produce nearly 30 million tonnes of steel per annum. Now, it becomes the second largest industry of India after SAIL.


Headquarters: Mumbai

Revenue: 1.172 trillion INR (US$17 billion, 2016)

Founder: Jamsetji Tata

Founded: 25 August 1907, Jamshedpur

CEO: T. V. Narendran (1 Nov 2013), Karl-Ulrich Koehler (1 Oct 2010)

Subsidiaries: Tayo Rolls, Tata Sponge Iron, Mjunction, more

official Website :https://www.tatasteel.com/

Selection Process:

Company conduct recruitment process every year to select new candidates. The selection process of the company consists of 4 rounds. These rounds are as follows:

  • Short listing on the basis of percentage or CGPA aggregate in graduation.
  • Written Exam
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

Academic Criteria:

  • Must be B.Tech /B.E(MME, Mechanical, EEE, Chemical, Production)
  • 60% and above or more than 6.5 CPGA aggregate in graduation degree.
  • Education gap of not more than one year.
