L&T ECC

Larsen & Toubro Engineering and Constructions Company (L&T ECC) is one of the largest construction companies. The company has played an important role in India’s infrastructure development. Entire L&T construction is divided into 4 independent companies that are Building & factories, infrastructure, Power distribution & transmission and Metallurgical and material handling.


Industry Conglomerate

Founded Bombay, Bombay Presidency, British India (1938; 79 years ago)

Founders Henning Holck-Larsen, Søren Kristian Toubro

Headquarters L&T House, N.M. Marg, Ballard Estate, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Area served Worldwide

Key people A. M. Naik ( Group Executive Chairman), S. N. Subrahmanyan (Deputy Managing Director & President)

Selection Process:

The company conducts recruitment process every year to select new candidates. The selection process of the company consists of 4 rounds. These rounds are as follows:

  • Written Exam
  • Group Discussion
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

Academic Criteria:

  • Eligibility criteria are Top-5 from EIE dept., Top-25 from Mechanical, and EEE and Top-30 from Civil. For the written exam with minimum 60% marks in Class X and XII.
  • The candidate should have passed all the exams in a single attempt.

Interview Experience 1

The criteria was above  6.75 aggregate GPA till seventh semester with no live back logs and 60% 60% in Xth and Xllth/equivalent.

The aptitude test duration was of 90 mins and 90 questions  constituting sections of quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal reasoning and technical part. There were 30 questions in the technical part.

The major questions were from microprocessors, networks, Boolean algebra, analog circuits, communications. Don't be worried seeing this many topics. You just need a basic knowledge of all this subjects to get through the technical part. The other 3 parts were of 20 questions respectively. All of the sections were of moderate level and one could easily solve them. Practicing Rs Agarwal and questions on indiabix are enough to get them done. There was no sectional cut off for each section.

In quants majority of the questions were from probablity and speed time distance and work calculations. Psychometric test was also there but it was not compulsory to attempt it. Even I didn't attempt it. After aptitude out of nearly 400 500 students nearly 130 students were selected for group discussions.
The duration for GD was of 8 minutes and general and current trending  topics  were given for GD. Our panel got the topic 'India's fight against black money'.

In  GD the instructions were given as of what stuff they are going to see. The major concern was to stay on the topic without diverting it and speak out valid points without cutting others and communicating with confidence.

After group discussions about 80 90 students were short listed for technical interview.

During technical interview the  questions were mainly asked on final year project. So I would suggest to get every know how about your project.

Then there were questions on opams mosfets and diode circuits, filters, micro controller, thevenin Norton theorem. You should stay clear and confident. If you have good  domain knowledge, you can easily get through.

Finally.. here comes the HR round... All general questions. Tell me about yourself, hobbies , why lnt, why should I hire you, location problems, any plans for higher studies... In HR be calm coz u r almost there. Speak with confidence and be prepared with this general questions and do listen the presentation of lnt personnels carefully.

It's tough to wait after HR right.. finally the list was out on 4/03/2017 and I was one among the choosen 67 people who are going to be a part of L and T  technology services.

Interview Experience 2

First step of the process was resume shortlisting. Candidates were shortlisted based on their CGPA: top 40 (for civil), top 30 (for mechanical & electrical), top 15 (for ece) and top 10 (for automobile).

Next round was the written round (of 90 minutes duration), which comprised 2 sections: aptitude and english. Aptitude section comprised 60 questions and English section comprised 40 questions. All questions were of MCQ type and there was no negative marking, but time management was difficult. Also, sectional cutoff as well as total cutoff needed to be cleared in order to qualify for the interview (technical and HR).

The technical interview lasted for 20-30 minutes. At first, I was asked to speak something about myself. Then the interviewer asked me which are my favourite core eng. subjects followed by the technical questions. Also, this is not an elimination round, which means that everyone was allowed to sit for the next round irrespective of how he/she has performed. Some of the questions asked in the technical round were:

   Question 1: How will you classify soil? (sieve analysis and IS soil particle size classification).
   Question 2: How to find the bearing & safe bearing capacity of a rectangular footing in sandy soil?
   Question 3: What is the difference between nominal and design mix?
   Question 4: What are the various tests on workability of concrete?
   Question 5: Draw the deflection diagram of a column (one end fixed, other end free). Also, how will you derive it?
   Question 6: How will you manage a project which is due 28 days? (related to gantt chart, CPM and PERT).
   Question 7: How will you test the compressibility of soil?
   Question 8: As per IRC, what is the maximum superelevation that can be used for the design of highways?
   Question 9: How will you cure a concrete structure if the available amount of water is less? (my ans: wet gunny bags) but what if the structure is very large (and use of gunny bags is not feasible)? (my ans: using special chemical admixtures in the design mix of concrete) but what if the concrete structure has already been erected? (ans: spraying of concrete curing compounds).

Next, the HR interview lasted for around 5 minutes. Basic questions like "What does your father do?", "Tell me something about your project", etc. was asked.

The HR mainly questioned me from my resume. He/she is trying to know whether I will stick to the company for long or will quit early. At last, I was asked whether I was comfortable with any location of work.

How long does it take to get hired from start to finish? What are the steps along the way?

The company then holds the test on a particular venue where close to 60 students each from each field, namely electrical, mechanical and civil backgrounds are invited. These students are first required to take a written test and then appear for two interviews. The selected candidates are then required to pass physical tests. After successful completion of the above they are sent to the company headquarters for their official designations and postings there after. The whole procedure takes around 6 months of time from start to finish.

What benefits does the company provide?

The company provided 1 month of Leave travel allowance every year excluding the 7 days paid leave.Health insurance, free lunch.

What is the interview process like?

The interview process was both technical and behavioral with more focus on behavioral aspects.

How are the working hours?

Total 9 working hours out of that 30 min. for lunch, 15 min for morning break, and 15 min for evening break
