Infosys

Infosys is an IT service provider headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. The company provides the clients with engineering, technology, business consulting and outsourcing services.

Today, Infosys is a global leader in consulting, technology and outsourcing with revenues of US$ 6.994 billion (FY12). Infosys provides business consulting, technology, engineering and outsourcing services to help clients in over 30 countries build tomorrows enterprise. Many of the worlds most successful organizations rely on Infosys to deliver measurable business value.

Infosys was co-founded in 1981 by "N. R. Narayana Murthy, S. Gopalakrishnan, Nandan Nilekani, N. S. Raghavan, S. D. Shibulal, K Dinesh and Ashok Arora"

Chairman Emeritus : N. R. Narayana Murthy

Executive Co-Chairman : S. Gopalakrishnan

Chief Executive Officer and Managing Director : S. D. Shibulal

Chairman of the Board : K. V. Kamath

Vision : "We will be a globally respected corporation."

Mission : "Strategic Partnerships for Building Tomorrows Enterprise."

Values : We believe that the softest pillow is a clear conscience. The values that drive us underscore our commitment to: Client Value, Leadership by Example, Integrity and Transparency, Fairness, Excellence

Awards : Infosys ranked among the most innovative companies in a Forbes survey Leading technology companies in The Boston Consulting Group Top ten green companies in Newsweek's Green Rankings.

official Website : http://www.infosys.com

Selection Process:

Infosys company conducts recruitment process every year to select new candidates. The selection process of the company consists of 2 rounds. These rounds are as follows:

  • Written Exam
  • HR Interview

Academic Criteria:

  • A minimum of 60 percent in class X and XII and 65 percent or above in B.Tech.
  • Candidates should not have more than 2 years gap in education.

Pattern of Written Exam:

Section No. of questions Time allotted (in mins)
Quant 10 35
Reasoning 15 35
Verbal 40 25
Total 65 95
Verbal Ability section

consists of short reading comprehension passages. This section also consists of basic grammar usage questions like fill in the blanks, synonyms, antonyms, paragraph completion, vocabulary, sentence correction, etc. The time allotted for this section is 35 minutes.

Analytical & Logical Reasoning

section consists of questions based on

  • data sufficiency
  • visual reasoning
  • data interpretation
  • syllogism
  • blood relations
  • statement reasoning
Quantitative Aptitude

section consists of questions based on

  • permutation & combination
  • number series
  • formulae
  • analytical puzzles
  • algebra
  • probability

Interview Experience 1

if you are about to sit for Infosys then focus on your aptitude. No need to bug much for technical part. I think its aptitude round is toughest in compare to other mass recruiters.

Infosys visited my college on 27th Sept. The day started with the PPT (Pre-placement talk). It lasted for more than an hour. Here the HR talked about the company, gave some data & figures of its company illustrating the rise of company from zero to zenith.

Then it was the time of online test which comprised of
> Quants- 10 ques - Level of questions is medium - 35 min. What I feel its wrong to say it quants section because there were 5 questions based on a complex arrangement, which is, of course, a part of reasoning but here it was in quants section.

The rest 5 were simple quants questions. You can easily solve them if you are even a bit serious.

> Reasoning- 15 ques - Level of questions is easy to medium- 25 min.

> Verbal- 35 ques -  Level of questions is medium to difficult- 30 min

Result came through mail on same day and 125 were selected among 400 students. Next it was the interview. My chance came very early as they to call students alphabet wise.

Me: Good morning sir.
Interviewer: Good morning, sit down.

Interviewer: Introduce yourself.
Me: Told.

Interviewer: Which subjects have you prepared?
Me: C, DBMS, OS.

Interviewer: What about Java? Don't you know Java?
Me: I know but I am not very much confident about it.

Interviewer: Tell me the difference between procedural & object oriented language.
Me: Told.

Interviewer: Write a program of overriding?
Me: Wrote.

Interviewer: What is inheritance?
Me: Told.

Interviewer: What is DBMS?
Me: Told.

Interviewer: Properties of DBMS.
Me: Told.

Interviewer: Stages of SDLC.
Me: Told.

Interviewer: Write a C program to count the number of Strings.
Me: Wrote.

Interviewer: Any question?
Me: Asked.

Interviewer: Explained.
Interviewer: Okay, now you can go.

Result came on same day and I was selected. Out of 125, 90 students were selected. In interview what you need is confidence, while giving answers if confidence is reflecting from your face then you are almost there. And of course luck you can't ignore the luck factor, it plays a good role in interview.

Interview Experience 2


Infosys consists of 2-3 rounds in their selection process. Aptitude and 1 or 2 HR rounds.

Round 1: (Aptitude).

Infosys Aptitude is assumed to be one of the toughest aptitude among the mass recruiters. To clear this aptitude R.S Aggarwal is not sufficient. Aptitude consists of 3 sections. They have their own portal for the aptitude.

1) Data interpretation and Arithmetic (Graphs, Probability, P&C).
2) Logical (Arrangement problems, Puzzles).
3) Verbal (Same topics as per verbal section on indiabix.com).

In this aptitude, time is sufficient and more emphasis is given on the logical thinking rather than the put value and get the answer. So practice more and more aptitude papers on web for Infosys.

Result: Cleared ;).

Round 2: HR interview. (90% HR + 10% technical)

Me: May I come in.
Int: Yes, Have a sit.
Me: Goon Morning.

Int: Goon Morning. (Going through my resume)
Your marks are consistently decreasing from 10th. Have you stopped studying?
Me: Explained how knowledge of current affairs and technology is important along with academics and also explained my participation in extracurricular (Guitar, programming languages (As I am from E &TC background)).

Int: How many Engineering colleges in your campus? What is the strength of E&TC branch in your college?
Me: Told. (Just answered some random figures confidently)

Int: What is diff. between object and function-oriented language.
Me: Told.

Int: What is polymorphism?
Me: Told. (Answer and justify it confidently, they will try to confusing you)

Int: Are you sure that answer which you have given is right?
Me: Yes sir. (Given example for polymorphism)

Int: (Again Going through my resume) You told your guitarist, but not mentioned participation of any event in resume.
Me: Told. (Differentiated among the Hobby and priority of career)

Int: Why IT company? (Most common question for non IT/comp people, be prepared for it)
Me: Told. (Field of interest)

Int: Have you already selected or seated or any any other company?
Me: No sir, This is my first interview. (Though I was selected in Cognizant) Sometimes you need to lie to stop the chain reaction of cross questions.

Int: Ok, and which are the upcoming companies?
Me: Accenture.

Int: Do you have any friends/seniors in Accenture?
Me: Yes sir. I have my seniors placed in Accenture but they didn't get joining date yet,;) (Again closed the topic)

Int: And, What is Package Accenture offering?
Me: 3.5

Int: And What Infosys is offering?
Me: 3.25

Int: Then why do you want to join Infosys ? Go for Accenture.
Me: (Smiled in cheeks) Justified as a Fresher package doesn't  matters. Package is second priority. Experience, Training Platform provided by Infosys is different than other companies. Also told I have my cousins working in Infosys told me about the working environment in Infosys

Int: Ok, Now give me an honest answer, I am interviewer of Accenture. Now tell me which company do you want to join?
Me: (With serious expressions,) Still I will join Infosys. After all, It's Indian Originated company and it was my dream to work with Infosys, which I was decided before appearing to the placement drive :)

Int: (Laughed) Ok. Do you know what exactly Infosys do?
Me: Told. (This question is another form of {What do you know about the company?}. Go through website of company at least night before appearing for interview).

Int : Any Questions from your side?
Me: Asked about the effects of Brexit (Current topic of that time) on the IT industry.

Int: Told.
Me: Ok. Thank you.

Done.

After waiting in class room for half hour, person came and read some names (My name was also there) and told them to leave and others asked to wait for 2nd round of interview.

I was  shocked But after a week  when results displayed. I was Selected  
2nd round of interview for those who failed to convince them in 1st.

Result: Selected and Placed.

Moral: If you clear Infosys aptitude, you are 90% selected. HR round is just to check your confidence and communication skills. Just stick to your answer, also be prepared for all questions related about your BE project (some of my friends asked about it) and show them that you are open to learn new things. That's all.

All the best..!!!
Tital

What is growth in terms of salary at infosys after probation period completion?

In Infosys you can get 7 to 8k increment after competion of your probation period.

What is the starting salary of freshers in infosys after training?

After training maximum package is 3.25 lpa. Based on the performance in training, Trainees are dividend into 4 catagories
  • TPF(<=92%) : Salary is 25400
  • HPF(>=80% and < 92%): salary is 24500
  • APF(>=70% and < 80%): salary is 23500
  • No performer: salary is minimum , 23000.
Note: if one fails any exam , he/she can not be TPF or HPF.
