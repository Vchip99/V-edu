TCS

Tata Consultancy Services is software services consulting company headquartered in Mumbai, India. It is the Asia’s largest IT service provider. The company also provides business solutions and outsourcing services.

Service : They provide service to varied clients for solving their business needs. TCS employees are outsourced for that work means, they are required to support & help their clients in solving their problems.

Consulting: It is the business model wherein TCS is being consulted by clients to engage in the business of giving expert advice to them so that they can grow either themselves or use option

Business Solutions : It is a combination of ideas used to help a company achieve its objectives. A business solution comes in terms of marketing, auditing, accounting market research, payroll and analysis, among other essential business activities.


Quick facts about TCS:
  • TCS is one of the largest Indian companies by market capitalization $80 billion.
  • In 2015, TCS was ranked 64th overall in the Forbes World's Most Innovative Companies ranking, making it both the highest-ranked IT services company and the first Indian company.
  • TCS is now placed among the ‘Big 4’ most valuable IT services brands worldwide.
  • As of December 2015, it is ranked 10th on the Fortune India 500 list.
  • TCS is the world's 10th largest IT services provider, measured by the revenues.
  • Recently TCS also become the most profitable Indian Company surpassing the Reliance.
  • TCS has been ranked number #1 in the client satisfaction in the United Kingdom.
  • TCS is the second largest employer of India after Coal India Ltd and the largest in the IT Services. TCS has more than 300,000 employees among them 31% are women. It also has 7.7% Non-Indian employees.
  • TCS is present in 46 countries and has 230 offices throughout. There are 147 delivery centers as well in 21 countries for the IT giant. Along with all these, TCS has 58 subsidiaries companies present in different locations
  • TCS was founded in 1968 to support the sister concern TISCO now Tata Steel. It also has two other clients Central Bank of India and Unit Trust Of India at that time.

Selection Process:

TCS conducts recruitment process every year to select new candidates. The selection process of the company consists of 3 rounds. These rounds are as follows:

  • Written Exam
  • Technical Interview + Managerial Interview
  • HR Interview

Academic Criteria:

  • Minimum 60 percent throughout the degree.
  • No backlogs should be there.
  • Gap in studies for upto 2 years is allowed with valid reasons.

Pattern of Written Exam:

Section No. of questions Time allotted (in mins)
Written Ability 1 10
Aptitude 30 80
Total 31 90
Written Ability

section consists of a scenario with certain keywords / phrases which should be used in the body of the given email. The minimum word limit should be 70 words. There is no negative marking in this section.

Aptitude

Aptitude frequently consists questions based on

  • Percentages, Profit and loss
  • Algebra, Ratio and Proportions
  • Permutation and Combinations, Probability
  • Time, speed, distance, Time and Work
  • Functions, Geometry
  • Numbers

Interviw Experience 1


1) Online test and email writing .
2) Technical round.
3) Managerial round (MR).
4) HR round.

1) Online test and email writing:

Soon enter or start the test email portal will open. Must include all the keywords, careful about the spelling mistakes and make sure that you mentioned the names in correct places as they have given in the question. Don't take this email writing as lightly, because even this considering as an elimination round so be careful.

After email, aptitude online test will commence. Here you will find totally 30 questions will be there (28 questions carry 1 mark each and another 2 questions of 5 marks each). Make sure that you will answer at least 18 to 20 questions correctly so that you will be on safer side. Negative marking will be there.

Questions appeared for me during online test is each question based on coding and encoding, blood relation, averages, set theory, directions, geometry (areas, volumes), permutations and combination, probability, calendar, time and work, syllogism, clocks. (only 87 members were cleared online test out of 450 members, thank god even I am 1 among them).

2) Technical interview:

For CS and is they will ask most on data structures, C++ concepts, basic C programs. And they will question depend on your resume and about projects. (in detail and role of you to your project).

Questions that interviewer asked for me.

- Tell me about yourself?
- Explain me about your project?
- The role of you towards your project?
- What is your favorite subject? (I said DBMS, few questions based on it).
- What are differences between C++ and Java?
- Is this your first interview?
- Being a topper why you didn't place in other companies?
- What are your hobbies?
- Who is your favorite singer?
- Have you any questions? (please do ask?).

3) Managerial round (MR).

- Tell me about yourself?
- Brief me about your project?
- He asked me one puzzle!
- What is success according to you?
- What is the difference between character and reputation?
- What you know about recent technologies?
- What you know about IOT (internet of things)?
- What is the most memorable moment in your life?
- What quality have you or describe be me about you in one word?
- What will you do if your trainer feels as you are dishonest? how will you convince him?
- What is goal?
- Where you want to see yourself after five years?
- Why should I hire you?

4) HR interview:

- Tell me about your project?
- What is your contribute to your project?
- How many members were there in your project?
- Who contributed more to your project?
- Any active backlogs?
- Will you ready to sign for two years bond?
- Have you any queries?

(please do ask questions).

At 7'o clock, we got results and my friend informed me that I have selected as such, it was such a memorable moment in my life.

Out 87 members only 46 members were selected (thank you so much, god).

Interviw Experience 2

1) Aptitude round: They have a definite pattern for this. Chances are there that you may also get repeated questions from previous year paper. So follow websites like IndiaBix blindly. Yes blindly because TCS Apti round has negative marking and you will not go for the answer until and unless you are 100% sure about it. So better to solve questions on these websites specifically given for TCS.

You are 70% through if you clear aptitude round. :) :).

2) Technical Interview: Prepare for basics and by basics, I mean simple programs like binary search, terminologies, and simple logic they ask for getting highest no. between 3 nos. (they asked me this).

A simple query for DBMS part. For some of my friends, they asked scheduling in OS. Don't worry even if you don't answer its ok but then be sure to answer other questions. 2 3 wrong answers won't do damage. This round lasted for 10 minutes that's it.

Prepare your project very well. That's the strength you have in this round. Once started don't stop until the interviewer interrupts. Be thorough with your project implementation.

P.S: Don't worry. This is an easy round too and remember to be confident.

3) Managerial Round: Family background questions, introduce yourself and in some case a puzzle or a situation. Easy one.

4) Personal Interview: If you have made till this round  you are 99% in. 1% less because you will have to wait for final result announcement if you feel you were as terrible as our ex-prime minister and didn't speak a word. Luck matters too.

Ok now the questions:

- Introduce yourself (start with "MYSELF". felt he liked this word :) :) )

A situation : nuclear explosion has eliminated human race from planet earth. You are the only one surviving. What will you do?
                 
Alone on remote island. How to survive and seek for help. Family Background questions. Ready to work anywhere anytime?
     
- Why TCS? (Note: Never start with "AS YOU KNOW TCS IS". I did and the interviewer said "I know everything about TCS, you tell me why TCS for you"... :P )

All this took a day to finish off. Finally got selected.All the very best people. It's easy to crack TCS. All they look for is confidence. So don't be nervous whatsoever. Remember if you think job is important you will definitely feel low. Feel as if you are the most important entity and they are there for you

How hard is it to get into TCS through campus placements?

Getting placed in TCS is very easy if you will follow proper study strategy for one or two months before campus placement.

How would life be after joining TCS through campus placements?

It would be a great experience. Once you join TCS ,the first 3 month is "Training period" where you will learn a lot of things apart from technical. You will have interesting sessions where you will have to perform skit and various other group activities. You will be given various responsibility like Group Representative , Safety Champ and many more. The best moment would be the last working day of your first month when you will receive the message XXXXX Rs has been credited to your account and it will make you feel more Happy , Proud and responsible. After completion of training you will be assigned to a project where you will have to adjust accordingly in various fields which you will know only after getting into project.
